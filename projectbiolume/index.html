    How to play

    • Move the jellyfish up/down to dodge enemies.
    • Use Up/Down, swipe up/down or use W/S to move. Click or Space to shoot.
    • If an enemy hits you or the wall, you lose a life.
    • Powerups: Shield, Railgun, Multishot, +1 Life, pusher, stun-gun, 2x Multiplier.
    • Difficulty ramps up over time. Survive as long as you can.
